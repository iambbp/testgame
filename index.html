About The Game Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival RPG where players face endless waves of terrifying creatures. Collect ancient relics, upgrade your abilities, and team up with other survivors in this browser-based multiplayer adventure.

Monster Survivors Game Features

  • Real-time multiplayer battles
  • Over 50 unique monster types
  • Dynamic skill tree system
  • Cross-platform progression
  • Daily challenges & events

How To Play Monster Survivors

Use WASD to move and Mouse to aim. Collect power-ups to enhance your abilities and survive as long as possible!
